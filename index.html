What We Teach

Fitness

The number 1 priority!

A wholistic approach is taken to ensure all areas of the body are conditioned and a very high level of fitness is achieved through intensive cardio exercises.

Flexibility

Our number 2 priority.

There is a stretching regime in place at the club that is geared to aid short term gains in flexibility.

Techniques

Entails MMA (Mixed or Multi discipline) Development.

Students are prepared in the arts of Boxing, Thai Boxing, Kick Boxing, Wado Rhu Karate, BJJ and Wrestling.

Execution

Sparring (Practice Fighting) or Competition Fighting.

Our students are trained according to their indvidual needs.

Body mass index (BMI) is an estimate of body fat based on height and weight. It doesn't measure body fat directly, but instead uses an equation to make an approximation. BMI can help determine whether a person is at an unhealthy or healthy weight.
